Goals

In rough order of priority:
  • Secure
    • Establish un-alterable records, including records of title
      1Title meaning: ownership of a number of shares which can be divided, or merged when assigning onward.
    • Assignment of title
      2Assignment of title means recording a change of ownership of shares from the holder of one secret to the holder of a different secret.
      can only be accomplished with a secret that need never be revealed
    • Resistant to malicious confusion of records
  • Open, anyone can:
    • receive or assign title
      • receive title under a secret that only they know
      • propose assignment of title to any secret known/controlled by another
        3Assignment of title requires offer and acceptance, each identity (public key) can only receive title once, therefore it is required that the holder of the private key accept assignment of title under their public key.
    • obtain all records
    • verify validity of all records
      • title
        • chain of title
          4For any given title to shares, anyone can trace the source(s) of the shares back to the genesis block.
        • absence of double spend
          5confirm that title has not been assigned from the record of initial receipt of title through to the recording of assignment of title.
      • recording process
    • participate in the recording process
      6by running software that creates, validates, and proposes pages and blocks for inclusion in the blockchain.
    • examine the computer code that implements the system
  • Accessible
    • simple, clearly expressed processes, not un-necessarily baffling
    • minimize real-world cost of participation in any part of the process
    • serve the users of the system, not the owners of the infrastructure
  • Extensible
    • interoperability with other systems
    • designed for inclusion of or migration to new technologies when desired
    • tunable parameters to adjust to changing use conditions
As with any non-trivial project, several goals are in opposition. The unstated goal is to optimize tradeoffs making the system as valuable as possible to users. Users are the value in the system, the system should return as much of that value as possible to them, encouraging wider adoption and increasing total value.
